This internal document describes the professional training programs offered by the organization. All courses are designed for students and working professionals aiming to gain practical, industry-ready technical skills.

Each program has a fixed duration of six months. Course fees vary depending on the specialization and depth of training.

All programs emphasize hands-on learning, real-world practice, continuous evaluation, and skill development through structured activities such as mock tests, weekly presentations, workshops, and projects.

--------------------------------------------------

1. Data Science Program

Duration: 6 months  
Course Fee: 30,000 INR  

The Data Science program focuses on data analysis, statistics, machine learning, and real-world problem solving. Learners are trained in Python programming, data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, SQL, and machine learning fundamentals.

The course includes extensive hands-on practice using real datasets, regular mock tests to assess understanding, week-wise presentations to improve communication skills, workshops on applied data science use cases, mini-projects, and a final capstone project.

Duration: 6 months

The program provides hands-on coding sessions, weekly presentations, mock tests for technical assessment, and workshops focused on building real-world web applications.

Duration: 6 months
